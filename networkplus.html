About the Network+ Exam

This certification exam is designed to assess your knowledge of networking concepts including protocols, topologies, devices, security, and troubleshooting. The exam consists of up to 50 multiple-choice questions randomly selected from a broader question bank.

  • You must score at least 80% to pass and receive a certificate.
  • Each question has one correct answer.
  • You can navigate back and forth between questions.
  • Once submitted, you can review your answers and explanations.

Good luck! Click "Start Quiz" when you're ready.

Career Impact

The knowledge gained from this course equips individuals with a strong foundation in networking fundamentals such as IP addressing, routing and switching, network security, and network troubleshooting.

These skills are directly applicable to a variety of entry-level and intermediate roles in IT and cybersecurity, including:

  • Network Technician
  • IT Support Specialist
  • Systems Administrator
  • Junior Network Analyst

Completing the course and earning the accompanying certificate validates your technical competency and demonstrates to employers that you are prepared to contribute effectively in IT and network-based roles.
