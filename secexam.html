About the Exam

This certification exam is designed to assess your knowledge in core cybersecurity concepts such as firewalls, malware detection, encryption, and secure network design. The exam consists of up to 50 multiple-choice questions randomly selected from a larger pool.

  • You must score at least 80% to pass and receive a certificate.
  • Each question has one correct answer.
  • You can navigate back and forth between questions.
  • Once submitted, you can review your answers and explanations.

Good luck! Click "Start Quiz" when you're ready.

Career Impact

The knowledge gained from this course equips individuals with a solid foundation in essential cybersecurity principles, tools, and techniques such as threat detection, vulnerability assessment, incident response, and network defense.

These skills are directly applicable to a variety of entry-level and intermediate roles in cybersecurity, including:

  • Security Analyst
  • SOC Analyst
  • IT Security Administrator
  • Junior Penetration Tester

Completing the course and earning the accompanying certificate serves as a recognized validation of one’s capabilities and commitment to the field. This certification enhances a candidate’s credibility in the job market and provides employers with assurance of the candidate's practical understanding and readiness to contribute to a cybersecurity team.
